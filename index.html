Lugbe Extension, Abuja

A New Standard of Living in Lugbe Extension

These eight 2-bedroom apartments represent a rare combination of quality finishing, strategic location, and modern estate living — all within one of Abuja's fastest-growing residential corridors.

Each flat is thoughtfully designed with high-gloss porcelain floors, POP ceilings with recessed LED lighting, marble-effect bathroom tiles, and modern rain shower systems. Security is built in — steel entrance doors, burglar-proof window grilles, and a perimeter-fenced compound.

The estate sits on tarred, landscaped grounds with dedicated parking and the dramatic backdrop of Abuja's iconic rocky hills.

  • POP suspended ceilings with recessed LED downlights
  • Marble-effect bathroom tiles with modern rain shower + water heater

  • En-suite bathrooms with WC, wash-hand basin, bidet spray
